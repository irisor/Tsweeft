Tsweeft Chat Translator

How to use this extension
  1. Select the chat area: click the 'Select chat area' button below to select the conversation area on the webpage.
  2. Select the input area: click the 'Select input area' button below to select the text input field where you write your chat messages.
  3. Verify that your language and your partner's language are set correctly in the 'Language Selection' section.
  4. Your partner's text will appear in the 'Partner Text' field with its translation.
  5. Write your own answer in your language in the 'My Text' field; the translation will appear immediately in the 'My Translated Text' field.
  6. Click the 'Send' button to send your message.
  7. Return to the webpage, review your answer, and submit it.
This extension cannot interact with certain chat elements, such as those inside iframes.
